Services

Trauma-informed, faith-based support for women seeking healing and restoration.

Our services are centered on helping women find support, advocacy resources, healing community, and a renewed sense of identity and dignity.

Support

Compassionate, trauma-informed support for women who need a safe place to be seen, heard, and encouraged.

Advocacy resources

Guidance and connection to resources that can help women navigate difficult circumstances with clarity and support.

Healing spaces

Community-centered spaces designed to help women heal, grow, and reconnect with hope.

What this support looks like

Practical help with a deeply personal heart.

Daughter of a King Inc serves women who have survived domestic violence, narcissistic abuse, addiction, and trauma. We understand that healing is rarely linear and that support must honor both the reality of trauma and the hope of restoration.

  • Faith-centered encouragement and care
  • Trauma-informed understanding
  • Supportive community connection
  • Resources that help women move forward

Our aim

To help women rebuild their lives without shame and without having to heal alone.

Everything we offer is meant to point women toward restoration, truth, and the identity God has given them.
