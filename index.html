Our mission

Helping women break cycles, reclaim identity, and walk in the dignity God intended.

Daughter of a King Inc exists so women do not have to heal alone. We are building safe, faith-filled spaces where women can find support, truth, community, and a path forward.

Who we serve

A ministry shaped by compassion, wisdom, and lived understanding.

Women facing abuse

Support for women impacted by domestic violence and narcissistic abuse, with care rooted in dignity, safety, and truth.

Women recovering from addiction

Encouragement and community for women rebuilding their lives and seeking restoration after addiction.

Women healing from trauma

Trauma-informed support that recognizes the deep effects trauma can have on the mind, body, faith, and daily life.

How we help

Support that is practical, personal, and grounded in biblical truth.

Our work is centered on helping women find safe support, connect with advocacy resources, and experience healing in community. Everything we do is guided by the belief that restoration is possible.

  • Trauma-informed support and encouragement
  • Advocacy resources and guidance
  • Community events and healing spaces
  • Faith-centered care rooted in identity in Christ
