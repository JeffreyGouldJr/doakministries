Training and workshops

Equipping churches, leaders, and communities to understand trauma and support women wisely.

Daughter of a King Inc offers faith-based workshops and training designed to strengthen the way churches, ministries, and organizations care for women affected by abuse, addiction, and trauma.

Church leaders

For pastors, ministry leaders, and teams who want to serve women with greater compassion, understanding, and wisdom.

Community partners

For organizations and local leaders who want a clearer understanding of trauma-informed care and faith-based support.

Support teams

For those creating environments where survivors need to feel safe, honored, and supported.

Training themes

Topics that bring clarity, compassion, and practical direction.

  • Understanding trauma and abuse
  • Supporting survivors with wisdom and compassion
  • Creating safe spaces for healing
  • Restoration, identity, and biblical truth
